PHP Comments

The comments are used to make notes of what the coder or programmer is writing. It is used to let others who are working on a project to understand what his colleagues has done.
It also enables the programmer to understand or remind him of what he has done.

When the computer is reading a program, when it gets to the comment, it will pass without reading it. In other words, the comments are ignored during program execution.

There are two basic ways in which you can comment out some lines:
